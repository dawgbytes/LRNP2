About

Lumber River Native Plants grows Southeastern U.S. natives with a special focus on wetland, pond, and riparian species (including hardy water lilies). Plants are nursery-propagated.

Wholesale • Restoration • Landscape

Water & Wetland Natives

  • Hardy water lilies (e.g., Nymphaea odorata)
  • Marginal/emergent species for ponds & shorelines
  • Stream & floodplain natives for stabilization

Project Types

  • Habitat & shoreline restoration
  • Stormwater & rain-garden planting
  • Nursery wholesale & contract growing
